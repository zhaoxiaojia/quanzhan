◆ client_socket_sdp_uuid_found_callback

typedef void(* client_socket_sdp_uuid_found_callback) (aceBT_bdAddr_t *p_addr, aceBT_uuid_t *p_uuid, uint8_t uuid_count)

Callback to notify application about uuids of a bonded remote device
This is invoked after bonding is complete or after reconnection
After bonding, this callback is sent after middleware identifies UUIDs from SDP process
After reconnection, this callback is sent if the remote device's UUIDs are present in middleware DB

Parameters
[in]p_addrAddress of remote device
[in]p_uuidList of UUIDs
[in]uuid_countNumber of UUIDs found for remote device
